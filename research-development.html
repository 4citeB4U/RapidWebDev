Platform Overview

The Artist Empowerment Platform is a comprehensive ecosystem designed specifically for independent artists who want to maintain control of their creative work while maximizing their income potential. Unlike traditional label deals that strip artists of their rights and revenue, our platform puts you in complete control.

Core Philosophy

Our platform was born from the story of MacMillion, a talented artist who recognized the exploitative nature of traditional music industry contracts. Before he passed, he left behind a promise: to create a platform owned by artists, not labels‚Äîwhere musicians could tell their stories, connect directly with fans, and sell their art without being silenced or stripped of their value.

Our mission: To provide artists with a path to grow on their own terms, from a single-page Jump-Off all the way to a complete Brand ecosystem, with full ownership of their creative work and direct connections to their audience.

Platform Tiers

We offer four flexible tiers designed to meet you where you are in your career journey:

The Jump-Off

Our entry-level option perfect for artists just starting their journey. Get a professional one-page website with music embeds, merch options, email collection capabilities, and basic Agent Lee functionality.

The Influencer

Designed for artists building their audience. Adds reels, giveaways, sponsor & collab tools, and enhanced Agent Lee functionality with promotional capabilities.

The Momentum

For artists accelerating their growth. Includes fan engagement features like fanwalls and galleries, ad monetization tools, advanced layout customization, and full Agent Lee functionality.

The Brand

Our comprehensive solution for established artists. Multi-page design with NFT and merchandising studio, booking and CRM capabilities, and Agent Lee AI Concierge service for complete digital asset management.

Agent Lee can provide personalized recommendations on which tier best suits your current career stage and goals. Just ask "Which platform tier is right for me?" and answer a few simple questions about your career status, goals, and budget.
