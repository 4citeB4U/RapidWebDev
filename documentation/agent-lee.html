ü§ñ What is Agent Lee?

Agent Lee is an AI-powered digital assistant specifically designed for artists, serving as your personal guide to both the platform and the music industry at large. Agent Lee integrates directly into your digital platform, offering real-time assistance, industry insights, and powerful voice control of your content.

Unlike general-purpose AI assistants, Agent Lee has been built with a deep understanding of the music business, including the often hidden mechanics of deals, contracts, streaming economics, and fan engagement strategies. It's the equivalent of having a trusted industry insider available 24/7.

Agent Lee's Primary Roles:

  • Personal platform guide
  • Industry knowledge repository
  • Voice-controlled platform manager
  • Fan engagement strategist

üìä Industry Insights

Streaming Revenue Breakdown

One of Agent Lee's most powerful features is its ability to decode the complex and often opaque world of streaming revenue. This allows you to understand exactly how your music generates income and what you're actually receiving.

What Agent Lee Can Show You:

  • Real-time calculation of earnings based on your actual streaming numbers
  • Comparative analysis with industry standards and averages
  • Transparent breakdowns of platform-specific payout rates
  • How various intermediaries affect your final payout

Contract Clause Decoder

The music industry is infamous for complex contracts filled with legal jargon that can significantly impact an artist's career and earnings. Agent Lee demystifies these contracts, explaining their real implications in plain language.

360 Deal Translation

Understand the full implications of giving away percentages of all your revenue streams, not just music sales

Advance Payment Implications

Decode how advances work as loans against future earnings, not actual payments

Publishing Rights Explanation

Learn about the long-term value of publishing rights and how they generate revenue

Example: The Truth Behind a 10% Royalty Rate

When your contract mentions a "10% royalty rate," Agent Lee reveals what this actually means: After recoupable expenses, manufacturing costs, packaging deductions, and various holdbacks, your actual earnings might be closer to 2% of the retail price.

Agent Lee can break down exactly how a seemingly reasonable percentage can translate to pennies on the dollar in real-world terms.

üõ°Ô∏è Privacy & Data Management

Agent Lee is built with privacy and data security as core principles. Your information stays under your control, and the system is designed to function with minimal data exposure.

Local Data Storage

Agent Lee primarily uses IndexedDB for local data storage, keeping information on your device rather than external servers. This enables functionality while maintaining privacy.

No Third-Party Data Selling

Your information is never sold or shared with third parties. Agent Lee's business model is based on platform value, not data monetization.

User-Controlled Permissions

You control what information Agent Lee has access to through explicit permission settings that can be adjusted at any time.

Encrypted Communication

Any data exchanges that do occur utilize end-to-end encryption to ensure sensitive information remains secure.
