Step 2

Initial Setup

After selecting your tier, complete these initial setup steps to configure your platform:

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email address and create a secure password. You can also use social signin options for quicker setup.

Choose Your Domain

Select a custom domain name for your artist platform. You can use an existing domain you own or register a new one through our service.

Select a Template

Choose from our music-focused templates as your starting point. Each template is fully customizable to match your artistic vision.

Basic Profile Information

Enter your artist name, bio, and contact information. This will be used to personalize your platform and help Agent Lee provide relevant assistance.

Template Selection Tips:

Consider your primary genre and career stage when selecting a template. Each template is optimized for different goals - from getting gigs to selling merch to growing your fanbase.

Step 3

Content Upload

Now it's time to add your content to the platform. This is where you'll showcase your music, images, videos, and other media:

Music Management

  • Upload audio files or connect streaming services like Spotify, Apple Music, or SoundCloud
  • Organize tracks into albums, EPs, or singles with release dates
  • Add lyrics, production credits, and liner notes

Visual Content

  • Upload high-quality profile photos, album artwork, and promotional images
  • Add music videos, performance clips, or behind-the-scenes content
  • Create gallery collections for press photos, tour memories, or fan submissions

Performance Info

  • Add upcoming shows, tours, and festival appearances
  • Connect with Bandsintown or Songkick for automated tour updates
  • Configure ticket purchasing or RSVP functionality

Written Content

  • Create your artist bio, press kit, and about page
  • Set up a blog or news section for updates and announcements
  • Add testimonials, press quotes, or fan reviews

Agent Lee Can Help:

If you're struggling with content creation, ask Agent Lee to help generate artist bios, blog posts, or press kit materials based on your musical style and career information.

Step 4

Configure Agent Lee

Agent Lee is your AI-powered digital assistant specialized in the music industry. Customize Agent Lee to provide the best support for your specific needs:

Activation & Permissions

Activate Agent Lee and set appropriate permissions for what data it can access and what actions it can perform on your behalf.

Personalization

Customize Agent Lee's knowledge base with your specific music industry information:

  • Upload any existing contracts for analysis and insights
  • Connect your streaming accounts for revenue analysis
  • Provide genre-specific market data for targeted insights
  • Set your career goals to receive relevant recommendations
