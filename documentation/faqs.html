General Questions

Platform Tiers

What are the different tiers available?

The platform offers three tiers:

  • Essential: Basic website functionality with industry insights and core Agent Lee features
  • Professional: Advanced features including e-commerce, advanced analytics, and full Agent Lee capabilities
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for labels, management companies, and established artists with multiple sites and team collaboration features
Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier?

Yes, you can change your tier at any time. When upgrading, you'll immediately gain access to all features in the higher tier. When downgrading, you'll maintain access to your higher tier until the end of your current billing cycle, after which the platform will transition to the features of your new tier.

Is there a trial period?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of the Professional tier for all new users. This allows you to experience the full capabilities of the platform before making a commitment. No credit card is required to start your trial.

What happens to my site if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your subscription, your site remains fully functional. This is one of the key benefits of our single-file architecture. You will lose access to ongoing updates, Agent Lee features, and our hosting services, but the website itself will continue to work and remain in your possession. You can host it anywhere that supports standard web hosting.

Technical Questions

What is the single-file architecture?

Single-file architecture is our innovative approach that packages your entire website into a self-contained HTML file. This architecture:

  • Ensures you maintain complete ownership of your platform
  • Allows your website to function without dependency on external services
  • Makes it easy to transfer your site between hosting providers
  • Improves loading speeds by reducing server requests
  • Provides built-in offline capabilities
Can I use my own domain name?

Yes, you can use your own domain name with all platform tiers. We provide simple instructions for connecting your domain, regardless of where it's registered. The Professional and Enterprise tiers include domain registration and management at no additional cost.

Is the platform mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. All sites created with the Artist Empowerment Platform are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. Our templates and components are designed with a mobile-first approach, ensuring your fans have an excellent experience regardless of the device they're using.

What about SEO and social media integration?

The platform includes comprehensive SEO tools to help your site rank well in search engines. This includes meta tag optimization, structured data, and performance optimization. Social media integration is seamless, with built-in sharing capabilities, feed displays, and automated posting features in the Professional and Enterprise tiers.

Privacy & Security

How is my data protected?

We implement industry-leading security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Two-factor authentication for account access
  • Data isolation to ensure your information remains private
  • Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations

For more detailed information, please see our Privacy & Security documentation.

Who owns the content I create on the platform?

You retain 100% ownership of all content created on the platform. This includes your website, music, images, videos, and any content generated with assistance from Agent Lee. Our terms of service explicitly state that we make no claim to your intellectual property.

Can Agent Lee access my personal files and data?

Agent Lee only accesses the data you explicitly share within the platform. You control what information Agent Lee can use, and you can revoke access at any time through the privacy settings. We maintain strict data isolation practices to ensure your information is used only for the specific tasks you request.

Is my financial information secure?

Yes, all financial transactions are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment processors. We never store your complete credit card information on our servers. For the e-commerce features in Professional and Enterprise tiers, we implement additional security measures to protect both your financial information and that of your customers.
