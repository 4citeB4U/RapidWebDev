Getting Started Guide

Welcome to the Artist Empowerment Platform! This guide will help you set up your account and start building your artist presence.

1. Creating Your Account

To begin your journey on the Artist Empowerment Platform:

  1. Visit the registration page and enter your basic information
  2. Verify your email address via the confirmation link
  3. Complete your artist profile with your stage name, bio, and genre
  4. Upload a profile image and cover photo
  5. Connect your social media accounts (optional but recommended)

2. Setting Up Your Artist Platform

Once your account is created, it's time to build your platform:

  1. Select a template design that matches your artistic style
  2. Customize colors, fonts, and layout to match your brand
  3. Set up your storefront for music, merch, and digital products
  4. Configure payment options and payout methods
  5. Create your first release or product

3. Connecting with Fans

Building your fan community is essential for success:

  1. Set up your messaging preferences for email, SMS, and push notifications
  2. Create a welcome message for new fans
  3. Import existing fan contacts (if you have them)
  4. Configure your fan board settings and moderation preferences
  5. Plan your first fan-exclusive content drop

4. Working with Agent Lee

Agent Lee is your AI assistant throughout your journey:

  1. Access Agent Lee by clicking the robot icon in the bottom right corner
  2. Complete the onboarding dialogue to help Agent Lee understand your goals
  3. Train Agent Lee on your communication style and preferences
  4. Ask for assistance with any platform features or strategies
  5. Review Agent Lee's suggestions for platform optimization

Pro Tip: For the best results, spend at least 15 minutes training Agent Lee on your unique voice and preferences. The more Agent Lee learns about you, the more effective its assistance will be.

Platform Usage Guide

This guide covers the core features of the Artist Empowerment Platform and how to use them effectively.

Content Management

Upload and manage your creative content:

  • Music: Upload tracks in WAV, MP3, or FLAC format. Set pricing, release dates, and availability.
  • Videos: Host music videos, live performances, or behind-the-scenes content directly on your platform.
  • Images: Create galleries for press photos, album art, and visual content.
  • Blog/Updates: Keep fans informed with regular posts about your journey and upcoming releases.

Store Configuration

Sell your music, merchandise, and digital products:

  • Product Management: Add items, set pricing, manage inventory, and track sales.
  • Bundling: Create special offers by combining multiple products.

Fan Engagement Tools

Build and nurture your community:

  • Fan Board: Create a space for fans to interact with you and each other.
  • Messaging: Send targeted communications to specific audience segments.
  • Exclusive Content: Offer special material to subscribers or paying supporters.
  • Live Streaming: Host virtual events, Q&As, or performances.

Analytics and Insights

Track your growth and understand your audience:

  • Sales Dashboard: Monitor revenue from all sources in real-time.
  • Audience Metrics: Analyze fan demographics, geographic distribution, and behavior patterns.
  • Content Performance: See which songs, videos, and posts resonate most with your audience.
  • Campaign Tracking: Measure the success of promotional efforts and marketing initiatives.

Customization

Make the platform truly yours:

  • Visual Editor: Change colors, fonts, layouts, and design elements without coding.
  • Custom Domain: Connect your own domain name for a professional appearance.
  • Integrations: Connect with third-party services like Spotify, Mailchimp, or Google Analytics.

Voice Commands
